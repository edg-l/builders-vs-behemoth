Version: 0.1.6
Date: 2026-07-21
  Features:
    - Scripted arenas: each Builder now spawns inside a single-entry defensible pocket (cliff walls, with a water-moat fallback) so the Behemoth is forced to approach through one choke. Deterministic across multiplayer peers and regenerated each match.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Version: 0.1.5
Date: 2026-07-21
  Features:
    - Persistent currency balance shown in the top bar for players in a match.
    - Shop tooltips now show each upgrade's effect (Generator income, Wall HP, Behemoth stats) alongside its cost.
  Changes:
    - Builders now spawn scattered around a ring instead of stacked on one tile.
    - Anti-cheese: each side is restricted to its intended recipes. Builders can craft only the mod's structures; the Behemoth crafts nothing.
    - Native enemies (biters) are neutralized on match start so a third party cannot corrupt the match.
  Bugfixes:
    - Wall upgrade no longer destroys the old wall before the replacement is confirmed, which could leave a choke permanently open.
    - Guarded character spawning so a failed spawn can no longer crash the mod.
    - A match now requires at least two players to start, fixing a one-player soft-lock.
    - Late joins and disconnects during the countdown or a running match are handled instead of leaving ghosts or unreachable win conditions.
    - Eliminated players can no longer respawn back into the match.
    - Mod structures can no longer be blueprinted or robot-(re)built, which had bypassed tracking.
    - Upgraded Walls now mine back into a tier-1 item, closing a free re-placement exploit.
    - Added on-spawn role briefings, including a reminder that the Behemoth must hold its shoot key to attack.
